• Rules
    • Objective: Choose a hand (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) that beats your opponent's hand.
    • Winning Combinations: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.
    • Game Setup: Each player has a name and starts with 0 points; play continues until one player reaches the target points (e.g., 5 points).
    • Playing a Round: Players choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors; compare choices; winner gains a point; display results (name and choice).
    • Tournament Rules: Four players; two initial games (Player 1 vs. Player 2, Player 3 vs. Player 4); winners play in the final round; the winner of the final is the champion; games are played until one player reaches the target points (e.g., 3 points).
